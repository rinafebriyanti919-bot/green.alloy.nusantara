Who We Are

Building a Sustainable Future Through Scrap Metal Solutions

With a strong commitment to quality, reliability, and sustainability, we ensure that every product meets strict international standards. Our extensive network and efficient supply chain allow us to provide consistent volume, competitive pricing, and timely delivery.

Alumunium Wheel Scrap

Alumunium Wheel Scrap

High-quality aluminium wheel scrap sourced from used automotive rims. This material is clean, durable, and ideal for recycling into new aluminium products. Known for its excellent strength-to-weight ratio and corrosion resistance, it is highly valued in the secondary metal market. Perfect for smelters and manufacturers seeking consistent, premium-grade aluminium input.

Purity: 98%+
Brass Scrap

Brass Scrap

Premium-grade brass scrap collected from industrial and commercial sources. This material offers excellent conductivity, corrosion resistance, and reusability. Carefully sorted to ensure high purity, our brass scrap is ideal for remelting and manufacturing applications, delivering both economic and environmental value.

Purity: 97%+
Copper Milberry Scrap

Copper Milberry Scrap

Top-grade copper millberry scrap with high conductivity and minimal impurities. Recognized for its bright, clean appearance, this copper is widely used in electrical and industrial applications. It provides superior recycling efficiency and maximum return value, making it a preferred choice for refiners and manufacturers.

Purity: 96%+
Zinc Ash From Galvanising Industry

Zinc Ash From Galvanising Industry

By-product from the galvanizing process, rich in zinc content. This material is carefully collected and processed to maintain quality and consistency. Ideal for zinc recovery and reuse in industrial applications, offering a cost-effective and sustainable solution for metal recycling operations.

Purity: 99%+
Zinc Scrap

Zinc Scrap

Mixed zinc scrap sourced from various industrial components. This material is suitable for recycling and reprocessing into new zinc-based products. With good resistance to corrosion and versatile applications, it is an excellent choice for manufacturers looking for reliable secondary raw materials.

Purity: 97%+
Alumunium UBC Scrap

Alumunium UBC Scrap

Compressed and cleaned aluminium beverage can scrap, ready for efficient recycling. This lightweight yet high-value material is widely used in the production of new aluminium products. Environmentally friendly and cost-effective, UBC scrap is a key component in sustainable manufacturing and circular economy practices.

Grade: Industrial
